关于实验室

南京邮电大学机器感知与人工智能实验室(MASAAIL)隶属于南京邮电大学通信与信息学院，从事机器感知与人工智能的前沿研究。MASAAIL实验室各种处理图像的机器学习算法，创建了开源机器学习软件，并为医疗信息、大流量分析和其他科学领域的应用开发了强大的人工智能算法。

研究成果

MASAAIL实验室的研究解决了 Research in ILLIDAN Lab tackles a broad range of machine learning challenges, including but not limited to deep learning, sparse learning for high dimensional data analysis, transfer learning (multi-task and lifelong learning), information fusion from multiple data modalities, matrix completion and collaborative filtering, spatiotemporal analysis.

Learning from Multiple Tasks

Design learning formulations and optimization algorithms to learn multiple related machine learning tasks, performing inductive knowledge transfer among the tasks and improving generalization performance.

MALSAR Software Tutorial
Progression of Alzheimer's Disease

Build machine learning models that understand disease progression and identify signaling biomarkers, from multiple data sources, including medical imaging, genotypes, and other clinical information.

More Info
Biomedical Informatics

Develop efficient and effective machine learning techniques to build predictive models and perform computation phenotyping from large-scale electronic medical records.

More Info
Big Traffic Data Analytics

Analyze trajectories behavior from big traffic data and develop high performance predictive models for traffic status prediction and dispatch optimization.

More Info
Limnology

Develop spatiotemporal analytics tools to fuse heterogeneous data from different scales and model the dynamics of lake nutrients in United States.

More Info
Blockchain Research

Develop infrastructures and algorithmic foundations for efficient big data analytics on blockchain technology, especially on healthcare and network applications.
